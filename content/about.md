## About Us

Marine Food Products Co., Ltd is a professional manufacturer of premium marine food products. We specialize in frozen seafood, dried seafood, and processed marine products, serving customers worldwide with high-quality products and excellent service.

### Plant Size

Our company operates modern production facilities with advanced equipment and technology. We have multiple production lines for frozen products and dried seafood processing, ensuring efficient production and consistent quality.

### Quality Control

Our quality control laboratory is well-equipped and can independently conduct food safety testing. We strictly control the production process to ensure safe and hygienic food products. All our employees are professionally trained and certified.

### Our Market

We are a professional manufacturer of marine food products including frozen seafood, dried seafood, and processed marine products. Our products are exported to various countries and regions worldwide. We adhere to the business philosophy of "quality first, customer satisfaction" and are committed to providing high-quality marine food products to our customers.
